Mountain View, CA 83° May 18, 2022

Silicon Valley and PayPal Park

Today Giles had to go back to work so I woke up at 6 with him to drive him into work at Tesla's San Jose campus. It was pretty cool to see where he works and get to drive around the highways there, which are much wider and easier to navigate compared to Charleston roads. Sarah and I then went to breakfast at The Breakfast Club at Midtown in San Jose and it was really good. I got a HUGE bagel and we decided to go explore Apple and Googles campuses that were pretty close.

I was a littel dissapointed to find out that Apples incredible "Spaceship" campus is not accessible unless you have an insider who gets you a visitor pass. I had known about their impressive glass company headquarters and have always wanted to visit so it was a little bit of a let down that the only visitors center was essentially a really fancy Apple Store; plus the augmented reality tour of the building was closed that day so we didn't even get to explore the companies headquarters virtually.

Google, on the other hand was open and inviting to walk around. It was cool to see all the employees active around the campus, biking between buildings and sitting outside at their cafes. Sarah and I enjoyed walking around their campus for a good bit of time and pretending to recieve important calls regarding Google business; pretty sure we fooled a ton of employees into thinking we were one of them (we didn't). Sarah and I then went into Mountain View to walk around and get some bobba tea from one of the local restaurants and it was delicious!

We drove back into town to pick up Giles as he got off work and bring him back to his studio. Giles went to pick up some Chipotle while Sarah finished her homework and then we went to see a San Jose Earthquakes soccer game at their PayPal park. Gile's and our friend Oskar plays as a center back for their team but unfortunately he was not playing tonight. It ended up being a really entertaining game, ending 3-2 in favor of the Quakes after a come from behind win!
