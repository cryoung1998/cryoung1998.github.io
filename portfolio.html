Career Path

Wyman Gordon Summer 2019

Wyman Gordon in Dillon, SC was my first Co-Op in college. I worked with the manufacturing team to improve machining procedures on titanium forged components supplied to aerospace companies.

BMW Manufacturing January 2020 - August 2021

I worked with BMW for three Co-Op rotations with their Powertrain Analysis and Quality team. I helped determine root cause analysis of physical and electrical problems within pre-series BMW X-model powertrains.

Bosch Chassis Control January 2022 - Current

My first role with Bosch was as a manufacturing engineer, where I worked on process improvement and line efficiency problems. I am moving to the brake system endurance testing group as a C programmer using NI CVI.
